Liquid Chaos Tattoos and Body Piercing was opened in February of 2001 in Gibraltar Trade Center. Approximately six months
later I opened my second shop in Dearborn Heights, Michigan. In November of 2002, we sold the Gibraltar shop
in order to focus more on the shop in Dearborn Heights. Through the years, Liquid Chaos has had some amazing artists
in it's rooms, some of which have gone on to open their own successful shops, but all have on thing in common: They're the
best at what they do. I hold a very high standard for the artists I hire and we all strive to give the best possible experience.
Thank you for taking the time to check out our website, we look forward to serving you in all of your tattoo and body
piercing needs. Owner and operator, Jared "Bull" Ammons
